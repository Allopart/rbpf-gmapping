delta2tr

Convert differential motion to a homogeneous transform

T = delta2tr(d) is a homogeneous transform (4x4) representing differential translation and rotation. The vector d=(dx, dy, dz, dRx, dRy, dRz) represents an infinitessimal motion, and is an approximation to the spatial velocity multiplied by time.
